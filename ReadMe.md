# Nutrition X

## About 
Nutrition X is an app that uses the Spoonacular free API to help users discover recipes and track their daily nutrition. Users can create an account, browse thousands of recipes with photos, calories, and ingredients, and click into any recipe for full details including macros and step-by-step instructions. The app includes a Food Log to track everything eaten throughout the day, either by searching Spoonacular or entering meals manually. A Goals page lets users set daily targets for calories, protein, carbs, and fat with live progress bars that update as food is logged. A Calendar view gives users a monthly overview of their eating history, letting them click any day to review what they ate and their total macros for that day.
